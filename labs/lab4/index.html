Career and Mission



Devin has a B.S. degree in Computer Information Systems from the University of Dayton, with a focus in Cybersecurity and a minor in Communication. His commitment to and passion for problem-solving, communication, and self-development is the foundation on which he has built and maintains his drive to become the best leader, student, and community member he can be.
Further, Devin is an aspiring IT Security professional. Through such a position, he hopes to play an integral part in building a safer world for every user, everywhere. In so doing, he'll fulfill his desire to truly make an impact in the lives of others.

Background

Born and raised in the relatively small town of Owosso, Mi, I've always had a strong work ethic and great leadership skills. As the oldest child in a family of 8 children, I had my work cut out for me, and had to learn at a very young age how to gather many people around a single shared goal. Today, leading others is a skill I'm truly proud of. I completed my high school days at Lansing Catholic High School in Lansing, Mi, and attained my Bachelor's of Science in CIS from the incredible University of Dayton.

Where I've been, and where I'm headed

I've completed two internships with Sparrow Health System in Lansing, Mi, both in-office and remotely. This year, my internship extended throughout the 2020-21 school year, as I worked part-time in a software development role working in Network Automation and Software Development. For more information on that, check out my LinkedIn!

Soon, I'll be entering the workforce as a Technical Systems Analyst, focusing specifically in hospital paging systems and Network automation. From that springboard, I hope to leap into the world of ethical hacking, information security, and the dream of one day starting my own cyber security consulting company.
